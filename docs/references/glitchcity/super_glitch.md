Super Glitch describes a number of buffer-overflow related glitches (/wiki/Glitch) in the Pokémon games, and the access points for these corruption (e.g. "Super Glitch moves"), most commonly for Pokémon Red, Blue (/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Red_and_Blue), and Yellow (/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Yellow), but also in Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire (https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Ruby_and_Sapphire_Versions) and Emerald (https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Emerald_Version), Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen (https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_FireRed_and_LeafGreen_Versions) and possibly Generation IV (https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Generation_IV).
